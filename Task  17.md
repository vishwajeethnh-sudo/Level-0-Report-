## Virual Reality(VR) and Augemnted Reality(AR):
Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR) are emerging technologies that are transforming the way humans interact with digital environments. These technologies create immersive experiences that combine computer generated elements with real or simulated environments.

### Virtual Reality(VR):
Virtual Reality is  a technology that creates a fully immersive computer generated environment that users can explore and interact with. The user typically wears a VR headset which blocks out the physical world and replaces it with a digital environment. These are typically used in gaming, training and simulation, education, etc. 

### Augmented Reality(AR):
Augmented Reality enhances the real world by adding digital elements such as graphics, animations, or data to the user's view. Instead of replacing reality like VR, AR adds digital information on top of the physical environment. The basic appliactions of these are Retail, navigation etc. The most recent update of this was Apple's Vision Pro. 

## Difference Between VR and AR:
The Major differnece between these is the environment which it enhances the User Experience by making the world completely virtual or just a slight touch of digital overlays. 

The devices used are also different. In VR we use a headset and a joystick to experience the virtual world while in AR we implement technology with real world using smartphone, smart glasses etc.

User interaction in VR is inside a virtual world whereas in in AR is interaction with both real world and digital elements. 

## Technological Stacks:
These tyoe of devices require a lot of technology to build including Hardware software and supporting tech. 
### Hardware:
These include VR headsets, AR smart glasses, motion controllers, sensors, cameras, GPUs etc.
### Software:
Commonly used software tools are Unity 3D, Unreal engine, WebXR, ARcore, ARkit etc. 
### Supporting technologies:
This includes Computer Vision, 3D modelling, Artificial intelligence, motion tracking etc. 

## Trends in VR and AR:
The VR and AR industry is growing rapidly due to advances in computing, artificial intelligence, and graphics technology.

Growth of the XR Market: The global AR/VR market is expected to grow rapidly, reaching hundreds of billions of dollars in the next decade.

Industrial Metaverse: Industries are adopting immersive technologies to create digital twins, remote collaboration tools, and virtual training systems.

AI-Integrated XR: Artificial intelligence is being integrated into AR and VR to improve object recognition, voice interaction, and automation.

Spatial Computing: New platforms allow users to interact with digital objects placed in real space.

Education and Training: Educational institutions are increasingly introducing AR and VR labs to help students visualize complex concepts.

Immersive Audio and 6DoF Experiences: New technologies like MPEG-I immersive audio allow sound to change dynamically based on user movement, increasing realism in AR/VR environments
